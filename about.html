Community map of Armenia

The Comminity Map is a website for map-based access to spatial data from Armenian Open Data Comminity. It is an initiative of the Open Data Armenia

a>

The Comminity Map is a fully open architecture. When you access data through it, you are typically accessing the data directly from our data catalog.

The Comminity Map...

  • provides easy access to authoritative and other spatial data to government, business and the public
  • facilitates the opening of data by federal, state and local government bodies
  • provides an open framework of geospatial data services that supports commercial and community innovation

To see what data is available on the Comminity Map, refer to the Data Catalogue in the Comminity Map itself. Click the Data Catalogue tab and expand the Data Set group names.

How to use the Comminity Map

Just go to http://maps.opendata.am. Most operations are obvious, but you can click the Help and FAQs button near the top of this page for specific instructions.

For further information email opendata@opendata.am. Feel free to send us feedback and bug reports.

Contributing Data to Comminity Map

Please feel free to write as opendata@opendata.am if you would like to add your data to the Community Map.
